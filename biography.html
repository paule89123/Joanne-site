Biography

From pop to classical to jazz, Joanne’s exceptional vocal skills make her one of the most sought after performers in London. A graduate from the Guildhall School of Music, Joanne can adapt her performance to suit any ambience and venue. She can perform as a soloist and as part of a duo and has an extensive repertoire which is perfect for wedding ceremonies and after party entertainment.

She has performed both internationally, and at favoured London haunts: Ronnie Scott’s, the Barbican, Southbank Centre, Hayward Gallery (for Sir Anish Kapoor) and St Pancras Renaissance Hotel.
